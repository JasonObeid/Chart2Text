 This statistic represents the best-selling mid-size luxury car models in the United States in 2019. That year, around 161,000 Tesla Model 3 vehicles were sold to customers in the United States. Model 3 sales increased by around 14.8 percent in 2019, compared with the year before. The Model 3 is an all-electric sedan manufactured by Tesla Inc.
