 This statistic shows the number of victims of the worst mass shootings in the United States between 1982 and December 11, 2019. Shown are the 25 mass shootings with the highest number of fatalities. The number of injured people in these shootings is also provided. On October 1, 2017, Stephen Paddock attacked a crowd of concert-goers on the Las Vegas strip, killing 58 and injuring 546 others. He took his own life before authorities could apprehend him.
