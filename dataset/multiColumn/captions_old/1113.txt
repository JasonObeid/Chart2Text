 This statistic shows the annual mileage for diesel cars in England in 2014 comparing both age and trip purpose. Business trips covered the least amount of miles out of the various trip types with other private reasons had the highest share. Cars aged up to three years tended to travel the furthest annually with the older cars travelling less. Overall, diesel vehicles covered the furthest distances mostly when being used for private purposes, with the only exception being vehicles three years old or less, which travelled further for commuting purposes than for any other reason in the year 2015.
