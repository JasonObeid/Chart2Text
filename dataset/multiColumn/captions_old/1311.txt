 This statistic shows the average cost of various male hair and beauty treatments in salons in the United Kingdom from 2012 to 2017. Over the last three period, the average price of male hair cuts dropped from 19 British pounds in 2012 to 12 British pounds in 2014. The most expensive treatment that can be had for males in a beauty salon is the Facial or massage, which both cost 33 British pounds in 2017.
