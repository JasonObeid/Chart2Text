 Broadcasting is the largest source of revenue for Arsenal FC. In 2018/2019, the club earned approximately 210.6 million euros from broadcasting, more than double than in 2010/2011. The second biggest revenue stream is the commercial stream. In 2018/2019 the commercial stream amounted to 125.8 million euro. The English Premier League generated the highest projected revenue among the “Big-Five” European leagues in 2019. The English Premier League constitutes the highest level of professional football in England. It is considered to be the most-followed and best-known football league in the world. The Premier League in its current format, comprising 20 clubs, was formed in 1992, breaking away from the Football League, which was originally founded in 1888. Amongst the so-called “Big-Five” leagues in Europe, which include the top-tier football leagues of England, Spain, Italy, Germany and France, the Premier League generates the highest revenue with around 5.65 billion euros in projected revenue in the 2018/19 season. Arsenal ranks on fifth place in average annual first-team player salary in the English Premier League. In 2018/2019 the average annual first team player salary in Arsenal was 4.85 million British pounds. The first-team players in Manchester United received the highest average annual salary of 6.53 million euros.
