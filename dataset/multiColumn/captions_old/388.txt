 This statistic depicts the number of regular and temporary employees working for Monsanto as of August 2013 to August 2017. The agrochemical company employed 2,800 temporary workers worldwide as of August 2017. Monsanto was an agricultural company specialized on genetically engineered seeds. The company was headquartered in St Louis, Missouri. In September 2016, Bayer finalized a deal to take Monsanto over, which was completed on June 7, 2018.
