 The commercial revenue stream is the largest source of revenue for Internazionale Milano. In 2018/2019, the football club earned approximately 154.5 million euros from sponsorship and merchandising, approximately three times what they earned in 2009/2010. The biggest revenue stream was broadcasting – both from domestic and international competitions. In 2017/2018, Serie A ranked fourth in match attendance with a total of 9.4 million attendees. Internazionale Milano had the third highest market value among Serie A teams. In 2019, Internazionale was estimated at 557.4 million euros in market value. Ranked first and second were Juventus FC and SSC Napoli with approximately 782.5 and 563 million euros. During the 2018/2019 football season, Juventus paid the second highest average annual salary to its’ clubs players out of all teams in the Serie A with an average of roughly 4.88 million U.S. dollars.
