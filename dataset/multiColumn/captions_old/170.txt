 This statistic shows the median household income in the United States from 1970 to 2014, by income tier. In 2014, the median household income for the middle class stood at 73,392 U.S. dollars. This study defined middle class income households as those with an income between 67 and 200 percent of the U.S. median household income, after adjustment for household size. In 2014, the middle class income ranged from about 42,000 U.S. dollars to about 126,000 U.S. dollars per year for a three-person household.
