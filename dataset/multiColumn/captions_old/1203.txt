 This statistic shows the leading leisure activities consumers engage in weekly in the United Kingdom (UK) in 2014 and 2017. Watching TV was the most common activity listed, done by the majority of respondents in both years. More than a third of the respondents said they went to a pub on a weekly basis in 2014, although this figure had fallen by 5 percentage points in 2017 to 29 percent of respondents.
