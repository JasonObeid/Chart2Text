 This statistic shows the number of Bloomin' Brands, Inc. restaurants worldwide from 2011 to 2018, by restaurant brands. In 2018, there were 227 Carrabba's Italian Grill restaurants. Bloomin' Brands, Inc. is a U.S.-based restaurant company that owns and operates the casual dining chains Bonefish Grill, Carrabba's Italian Grill, Fleming's Prime Steakhouse & Wine Bar, Outback Steakhouse and Roy's.
