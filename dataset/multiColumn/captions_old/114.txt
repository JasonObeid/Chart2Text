Unstable oil markets Over the last few years, Helmerich & Payne, Inc. of Tulsa, Oklahoma has also been a major competitor, generating nearly double the revenue of Patterson-UTI in 2015 before the market severely oscillated during the 2010s oil glut. Prices were hit across the industry as a result of surplus supply, and revenue plummeted by 2016. Overproduction of shale oil in the United States strongly impacted the industry as new methods of extraction, such as hydraulic fracturing, came into more frequent use.
