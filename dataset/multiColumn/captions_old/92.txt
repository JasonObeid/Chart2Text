 High school sports in the United States has always been a huge industry and figures suggest that it is only getting bigger. During the school year 2018/19, 18,210 schools in the U.S. had a basketball program for girls, marking a steady increase on the figure from the 2009/10 school year. Indeed, growth was experienced across all major sports, with soccer and competitive spirit squads seeing some of the most significant increases in the number of programs.
