 This statistic gives information on the mobile data traffic worldwide from 2017 until 2022, sorted by region. In 2018, mobile data traffic in North America amounted to 1.8 exabytes per month. In 2022, North American traffic from mobile devices such as smartphones, laptops, tables, M2M connections and non-smartphones is projected to reach 5.85 exabytes per month. The projected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) for the region amounts to 36 percent.
