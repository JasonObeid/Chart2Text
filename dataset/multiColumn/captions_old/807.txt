 This statistic depicts the leading five purchasing countries of IKEA products from 2012 to 2016. In 2016, China continued to be the leading purchasing country of IKEA products at 26 percent. IKEA is a privately held, international home products company that designs and sells ready-to-assemble furniture such as beds, chairs, desks, appliances and home accessories. The company is the world's largest furniture retailer.
