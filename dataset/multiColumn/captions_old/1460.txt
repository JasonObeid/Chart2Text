 This statistic depicts the share of art buyers who bought art and/or collectibles directly online through a click-and-buy or click-and-bid (with or without having seen the physical object) in the 12 months previous from 2014 to 2019 (as of April each year). According to the source, 44 percent of respondents said they had bought art and/or collectibles online in 2019, up from 38 percent in 2014.
