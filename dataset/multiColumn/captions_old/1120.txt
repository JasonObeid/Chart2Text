 This statistic depicts the age distribution of the United Kingdom from 2008 to 2018. In 2018, about 17.68 percent of the population in the United Kingdom fell into the 0-14 year category, 63.93 percent into the 15-64 age group and 18.4 percent were over 65 years of age. The same year, the total UK population amounted to about 64 million people.
