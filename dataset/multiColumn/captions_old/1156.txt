 This statistic shows the value of the grocery retail market in the United Kingdom (UK) in the year to April 2014 and a forecast for the year to April 2019, by retail channel. The value of superstores and hypermarkets is predicted to drop from 73.7 billion to 70.8 billion British pounds in 2019, while the value of all other channels, particularly discounters and convenience stores, are expected to increase.
