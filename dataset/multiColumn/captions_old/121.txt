 As of March 2 in 2020, there was a total number of 254 confirmed cases of coronavirus (COVID-19) in Japan, with 232 patients showing some symptoms. Additional to this number, 705 passengers on the cruise ship Diamond Princess docked in Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture, were diagnosed positive with the virus so far. According to the source, all the passengers and crews got off the ship by March 1.
