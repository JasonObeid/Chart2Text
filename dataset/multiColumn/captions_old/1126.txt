Participation peaked in 2011/2012, when 22.8 percent of the children in that age group played rugby. The Rugby Football Union is the governing body for the rugby union in England. The number of people participating in rugby union has been declining. In 2018, there were 239,700 people who participated in rugby union at least twice a month at any intensity for any duration in England. In 2016, there were 259,600 playing rugby. In 2018, the most popular sport was running, followed by fitness with respectively 6.8 and 6.5 million monthly participants.
