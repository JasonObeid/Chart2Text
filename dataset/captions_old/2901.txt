 During the year 2018, the most sold video game on the physical market was FIFA 19 for the PS4, accounting for about 945.2 thousand units sold. In second place came Red Dead Redemption 2, with sales amounting to roughly 691.9 units. Video game market in France In 2016, the French gaming market has known a peak, with its value amounting to 2.3 billion euros, before decreasing to 1.6 billion euros in 2018. However, it appears that the online consumption of video games had been taking off during the past years: 34 percent stated they used online gaming in 2018 as opposed to 21 percent of respondents who consumed video games on the Internet in 2011. Video games usage in France In France, the percentage of people who play a video game at least once on an average day was the highest within the age groups from 13 to 24 years old and 25 to 34 years old: in these groups, respectively 42 and 30 percent of the surveyed played video games. It should also be noted that the share of women playing video games was of 18 percent in a survey from 2018.
