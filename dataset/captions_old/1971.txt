 This statistic shows the most dangerous cities in the United States in 2018, by violent crime rate. Four categories of violent crimes were used: murder and non-negligent manslaughter; forcible rape; robbery; and aggravated assault. Only cities with a population of at least 200,000 were considered. In 2018, around 2,007.82 violent crimes per 100,000 residents were reported in Detroit, Michigan. This made Detroit the most dangerous city in the United States.
