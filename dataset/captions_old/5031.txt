 This statistic shows the market share of the online travel agencies (OTA) in the U.S. in 2013. The OTA with the largest share of the U.S. market in 2013 was Expedia with 42 percent of the market. In 2015, only 52 percent of U.S. travelers completed their booking on the OTA site when searching for a hotel, with 49 percent preferring to use the supplier site.
