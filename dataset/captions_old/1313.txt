Motor industry contribution In 2018, the UK motor industry contributed 18.6 billion British pounds to the economy. It was the second year in a row that industry revenue declined, with 2016 being the peak year when contributions amounted to 22 billion British pounds. Car production volumes The decline in economic contribution is a direct result of a decrease in production output. In 2018, some 1.52 million passenger cars were manufactured. This was nine percent less than the volume produced the previous year and on a level with figures reported for 2013.
