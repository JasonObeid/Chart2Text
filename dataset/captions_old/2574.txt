Spotlight on Canada The house price to income ratio in Canada steadily rose between 2015 and 2017, but the ratio has remained steady since then. This is likely due to the cooling of the housing market since 2018 due to the recently introduced mortgage stress test, the foreign buyers tax in British Columbia and higher interest rates.
