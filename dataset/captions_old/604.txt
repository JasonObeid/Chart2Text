 As of February 2019, 67 percent of U.S. adults aged between 18 and 29 years used the photo sharing app Instagram. Furthermore, it was found that 43 percent of female adults in the United States used Instagram compared to only 31 percent of adult men. Instagram usage in the United StatesInstagram is one of the most popular social networks in the United States with a 37 percent usage reach among the adult population. Social media juggernaut Facebook has a 69 percent population reach.
