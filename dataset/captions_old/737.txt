In the third quarter of 2019, e-commerce sales accounted for 11.2 percent of retail sales in the United States. During that quarter, U.S. retail e-commerce sales amounted to over 154.54 billion U.S. dollars. Some retail categories are performing stronger in terms of e-commerce sales than others: in the apparel and accessories segment, 26.1 percent of total retail revenue was generated online. However, digital only accounted for two percent of U.S. grocery revenues.
