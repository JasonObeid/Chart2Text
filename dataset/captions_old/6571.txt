 This statistic illustrates the monthly number of customers who switched their current bank account provider in the United Kingdom (UK) between January 2016 to December 2019. It can be seen that the number of switches fluctuated during the period under observation, reaching a total of approximately 72.5 thousand as of December 2019. The largest number of switches in one month occurred in March 2016, when more than 124.6 thousand switches were recorded. The number of switches recorded in the previous twelve months (from the 1st of January 2019 to the 31st of December 2019) totaled more than one million switches.
