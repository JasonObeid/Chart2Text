 Beauty videos are big business on YouTube. In 2018, beauty-related content generated more than 169 billion views on the video platform. Popular types of YouTube beauty content include tutorials and DIY videos, reviews, haul videos and videos produced by beauty vloggers. Beauty content on YouTube/br> Fashion- and beauty-related content such as makeup and skincare tutorials, cosmetics hauls and product recommendations are among the most popular content on YouTube. Campaign films accounted for the biggest share of beauty content video views on YouTube, generating more than a third of views as of August 2018. Favorites or reviews and tutorials were ranked second and third with 15 and 14 percent of views respectively.
