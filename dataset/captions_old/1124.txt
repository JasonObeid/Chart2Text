 This statistic shows the Consumer Price Index for urban consumers for selected cities and metropolitan areas in the United States of America in 2019. In 2019, the CPI in U.S. cities averaged at 255.66. However, the CPI for the New York-Newark-Jersey City metropolitan area amounted to about 278.16. Prices in New York city were significantly higher than the U.S. average. The monthly inflation rate for the United States can be found here.
