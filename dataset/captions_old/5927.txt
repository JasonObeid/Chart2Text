Bicycle usage in France According to a survey conducted in 2018, the French seem to enjoy using bikes during their leisure activities, but not as much when it comes to use them as a mean of transport. In fact, while almost 9 in 10 respondents stated that they use a bicycle for leisure activities like hiking or diverse outings, only about one third declared that they use a bike as a mean of transportation at least once a month the same year.
