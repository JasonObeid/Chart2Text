 The United States is a major producer of soybeans worldwide, with China importing the largest amount of U.S. grown soybeans of any country as of 2017. In that year, China imported nearly 32 million metric tons of soybeans from the United States. Soybean production in the United States The United States produces the highest volume of soybeans of any country, at around 123.66 million metric tons in 2018/2019. In the previous year, the total value of U.S. soybean production amounted to about 39.13 billion U.S. dollars. Within the United States, the states with the most soybean cultivation are located in the Midwest. Illinois topped the list with over 698 million bushels of soybeans produced in 2018.
