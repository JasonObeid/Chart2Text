 This statistic ranks leading brands from all industries in Finland by average Buzz score in 2018. Global discount supermarket chain Lidl ranked number one with a score of 39.4, followed by Finnish food industry corporation Fazer and hypermarket chain Prisma. Prisma is owned by S-Group, a Finnish retailing co-operative, who also owns S-pankki (in English: S-Bank) that ranked fourth in the Buzz score ranking of leading brands in Finland. A dairy brand Valio ranked fifth, followed by Finnair, the largest airline of Finland.
