 This statistic displays the leading cheese brands in the United Kingdom (UK), according to a survey conducted in November 2013. Cathedral city and supermarket own label cheeses ranked highest, with the greatest percentage of respondents purchasing their cheese products at the time of survey. Retail sales volumes of cheese have increased year on year, with cheddar cheese the most popular type of hard cheese in the United Kingdom, mature cheddar in particular. Although the average volume of household purchases per week have decreased, average expenditure has increased.
