 This statistic illustrates the median annual gross salary for employees of the the financial and insurance sectors, as well as in real estate services in the United Kingdom (UK) in 2019, in British pounds. In that year, the highest median annual gross salary was from the financial services activities sector, amounting with roughly 42.1 thousand British pounds. Among all sectors in this statistic, real estate services presented the lowest annual gross salary, with roughly 25.9 thousand British pounds.
