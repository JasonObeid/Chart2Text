 The German car manufacturer Volkswagen employed over 644 thousand people worldwide in 2018, the most of any European company. The next largest European company in terms of people employed was the British foodservice company, Compass Group which employed over 595 thousand people, followed by Deutsche Post, which had 499 thousand employees. Oil giants lead in terms of revenue Although Volkswagen had the highest number of employees worldwide, it was only the third-largest European company in terms of revenue. The two largest European Companies by revenue were Royal Dutch Shell, and British Petroleum, which had a revenue of 396.5 billion US dollars and 303.74 billion US dollars respectively.
