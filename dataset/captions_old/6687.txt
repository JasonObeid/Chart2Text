 This statistic shows the number of Starwood Hotels & Resorts properties worldwide in 2015, by brand. There were 210 Four Points properties within the Starwood Hotels & Resorts portfolio in 2015. Starwood Hotels & Resorts was founded in 1969 and now has more than 1,200 properties worldwide. In 2014, Starwood Hotels & Resorts was the forth largest hotel group in the world in terms of revenue, followed by the Accor hotel group.
