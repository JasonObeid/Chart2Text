 The statistic represents the number of registered automobiles in California in 2017. In 2017, there was a total number of approximately 14.6 million automobiles registered in California. Automobile registrations in California In case you have ever asked yourself one of these questions: ‘How many cars are there in California?’ or ‘How many registered vehicles are there in California?’, we can tell you as much: In 2016, there were 14.5 million automobiles registered in California. Furthermore – as you might have wondered if other motor vehicles are registered in this state, as well - Californians used around 98,622 buses and coaches, over 14.5 million trucks and 842,106 motorcycles in 2016, making California the leading federal state in all motor vehicle segments.
