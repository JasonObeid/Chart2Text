 Between April and June 2018, the travel website OUI.sncf (previously Voyages-sncf.com) hosted the visit of about 12 million unique users per month. Travel booking websites OUI.scnf is the online ticket selling platform for rail travel of the French company SNCF, which is also linked to other European rail travel operator as Thalys or Deutsche Bahn for instance. Being accessible in many languages, OUI.sncf has become the main online selling point for railway travels in France.
