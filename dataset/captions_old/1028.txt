 In 2018, there were roughly 3.3 million German households with a household net income of under 900 euros per month. In total, there were approximately 40.4 million private households in Germany that year. Disposable net income Based on most recent figures, the largest number of households had a monthly net income of 3,200 to 4,500 euros. Food and housing When looking at private household spending in Germany, consistent patterns emerge. Housing, water, electricity, gas and other fuel made up the largest share, followed by food, beverages and tobacco. Spending for the latter has only been going up in recent years.
