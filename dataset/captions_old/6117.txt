Petroleum products refer to materials derived from crude oil and refined within oil refineries. Often, petroleum is converted into various classes of fuels, most commonly gasoline and fuel oil. Apart from common fuels, refineries can also produce chemicals that are often used in the production of plastics. There are a variety of by-products that are also derived from refining petroleum products such as fertilizer, perfumes, soaps, and vitamin pills.
