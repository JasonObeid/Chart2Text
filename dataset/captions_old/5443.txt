 This statistic shows the average age of the mother at the birth of a child in the Netherlands in 2018, according to whether it is the mother's first, second, third, or fourth child. It reveals that in 2018 women in the Netherlands on average were 29.9 years old when they had their first child. If they had a second child, on average it came a little over two years later, when they were 31.9 years old. At the birth of a 3rd or 4th child they were 33.3 and 35 years respectively.
