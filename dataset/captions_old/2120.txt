 The Fiat Ducato was the most common heavy goods vehicle (HGV) on the road in the United Kingdom during 2018. As of the fourth quarter of 2018, a total of 12,358 units had been registered. This was followed by the Iveco Daily and DAF Trucks Fa 45Lf. The number of HGV licensed Fiat Ducato vehicles was at their highest in 2018, having increased steadily since 2000.
