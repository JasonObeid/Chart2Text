Twitter has been slow to catch on in the NFL, most likely due to the strict social media policy adapted by the league. Mobile device and social media usage by coaches, athletes and team personnel has officially been banned during specific times before and after as well as during the game. Rules were relaxed during the Pro Bowl 2012 where players were encouraged to tweet during the game.
