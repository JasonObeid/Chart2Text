 This statistic presents the US states with the highest rates of electronic bullying among students in the grades 9 through 12. During the 2016-2017 survey, it was found that 19 percent of high school students in New Hampshire had been electronically bullied within the past 12 months. This includes being bullied through chat rooms, instant messaging, websites or texting. The U.S. average was 14.9 percent.
