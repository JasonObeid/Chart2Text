 This statistic shows the revenues of the U.S. government in the fiscal year of 2019. The total revenues received sum up to about 3.5 trillion U.S. dollars and consist of individual and corporate income taxes, payroll taxes and other taxes. Individual income taxes totaled up to 1.72 trillion U.S. dollars in 2019, which is approximately 8.1 percent of GDP, whereas corporate income taxes totaled to 230 billion U.S. dollars, about 1.1 percent of GDP.
