 In 2018, the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) staged a total of 39 events. However, after a considerable drop in Pay-Per-View buy rates in 2017, the average number of Pay-Per-View buys recovered to approximately 447 thousand per event in 2018. One major contributor for this was UFC 229 on October 6th 2018, featuring Khabib Nurmagomedov versus Conor McGregor, resulting in the top UFC event in terms of Pay-Per-View buys with a total of 2.4 million buys. Fighter earnings The highest all-time earnings directly through UFC fights were those of Conor McGregor at roughly 12 million U.S. dollars, however the majority of his personal income comes from other sources such as bonuses, endorsement deals, or for example his professional boxing match against Floyd Mayweather, for which he received over 75 million U.S. dollars. The earnings of fights depend heavily on the style card they fight.
