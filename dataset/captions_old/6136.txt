 The statistic illustrates the distribution of main merger and acquisition (M&A) transactions, by the deal volume, in the United Kingdom (UK) during the first half of 2019, broken down by business sector. It can be seen that the financial services sector was the leading business sector in the UK, with a total number of deals amounting to almost 1.2 thousand in the first half of 2019. The infocomms and manufacturing sectors completed the top three, with total merger and acquisition transaction volumes of 736 and 699 deals respectively.
