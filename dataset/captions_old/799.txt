 Royal Mail PLC is the United Kingdom's market leader in industrial transportation based on revenues. In its 2019 fiscal year, it generated 10.58 billion British pounds, which was far more than its nearest competitor - aviation service company Signature Aviation PLC. Established in 1516, Royal Mail PLC operated as a public postal service provider for most of its history, before the government sold majority shares following the Postal Services Act 2011. Market cap of UK logistics firms In terms of market capitalization, Signature Aviation PLC edged ahead of the Royal Mail. As of 2019, the London based company had a market value of 3.22 billion British pounds. Signature Aviation is the global market leader in aviation support and aftermarket services and operates largely in North America.
