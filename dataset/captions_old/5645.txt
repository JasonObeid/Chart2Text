 The statistic displays the rental prices per square meter per year of prime logistic properties in selected European cities as of the third quarter of 2019. Properties generally fulfill a number of criteria in order to be considered "prime properties". These criteria range from good location (in the major commercial centers), to public transport accessibility and energy efficiency of the premises. It can be seen that the most expensive prime logistic properties were rented out in Zurich, Switzerland. According to figures as of the end of the third quarter 2019, rental cost for prime logistics properties in Zurich reached a value of 230 euros per square meter per year. The cheapest rental prices for prime logistics properties, in the cities involved in this study, were found in Bucharest.
