As of May 2019 , Arya Stark earned the title of being Game of Thrones ' deadliest character , racking up an estimated 1,278 total kills . Dragons Drogon and Rhaegal ranked second and third with 508 and 273 estimated kills respectively , while Cersei Lannister ranked fourth with nearly 200 kills . Game of Thrones Game of Thrones is a fantasy television series which ran on HBO from 2011 to 2019 .
