The two Volvo companies , Volvo AB and Volvo Car AB seized the two top spots on the list of the top twenty companies with the highest turnover in Sweden as of February 2020 . Ranked first was Volvo AB , with a turnover reaching 390 billion Swedish kronor . Volvo AB ( also known as Volvo Group ) is a world leading manufacturer of mainly trucks , but also buses , and marine and industrial engines .
