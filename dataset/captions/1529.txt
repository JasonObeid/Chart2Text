This statistic shows a ranking of the most successfully completed Kickstarter projects as of July 2019 . At the end of August 2014 , the Coolest Cooler finished its Kickstarter run with more than 13.28 million U.S. dollars in project funding . Up until then , Pebble e-paper watch had held the title for the highest funded project on the crowdfunding website with 10.27 million US dollars .
