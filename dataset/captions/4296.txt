The German car manufacturer Volkswagen employed over 644 thousand people worldwide in 2018 , the most of any European company . The next largest European company in terms of people employed was the British foodservice company , Compass Group which employed over 595 thousand people , followed by Deutsche Post , which had 499 thousand employees . Oil giants lead in terms of revenue Although Volkswagen had the highest number of employees worldwide , it was only the third-largest European company in terms of revenue .
